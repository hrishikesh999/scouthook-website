Refund Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Refund Policy is provided by Copypower Digital LLP (“Copypower”, “we”, “us”) for the Scouthook product and related websites and services (the “Service”). Please read it together with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

1. What this policy covers

This policy explains how refunds work for fees you pay Copypower Digital LLP for Scouthook (for example, subscription fees for paid plans).

It does not govern fees charged directly by third parties (for example, taxes collected by a payment provider, payment processing fees charged by your bank/card network, or third‑party services such as LinkedIn).

2. Free plans

If you use a free plan, there are no payments and therefore no refunds.

3. Subscriptions (monthly and annual)

If you purchase a recurring subscription, your subscription will renew automatically at the end of each billing period unless you cancel before the renewal date.

How to cancel: you can cancel your subscription using the cancellation controls available in the Service (account/billing settings), or by emailing contact@scouthook.com with enough detail for us to locate your account.

What cancellation does: canceling stops future renewals. It does not automatically refund prior charges unless you qualify under Section 4.

4. 30‑day satisfaction guarantee (first successful payment only)

If you are a new paying customer and you are not satisfied with Scouthook, you may request a refund within 30 calendar days of your first successful payment for a paid plan.

What we refund: the subscription fee paid to Copypower for that first billing period, minus any non‑refundable payment processing fees, taxes, or government charges (if applicable), unless applicable law requires otherwise.

How refunds are paid: approved refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout.

Timing: refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days after approval, depending on your bank or card issuer.

5. Eligibility limitations (important)

To keep this policy fair and prevent abuse, refunds are not available if, before your refund request is approved, any of the following occurs:

  • Usage threshold: you have generated more than 10 posts in Scouthook. For clarity, this matches Scouthook’s usage counting today, including draft posts created in the product that count toward your plan usage/limit as shown in the Service.
  • Chargebacks / payment disputes: you have initiated a chargeback, payment dispute, or similar claim with your bank or card network for the same charge (we will respond through the payment provider’s dispute process).
  • Violation / abuse: we reasonably believe your account activity violates our Terms, applicable law, or third‑party platform policies (including LinkedIn), or involves fraud, chargeback abuse, or unauthorized access/resale.

If you are unsure whether you qualify, email contact@scouthook.com and include the email address on your account and your approximate purchase date.

6. Renewals after the first payment

The guarantee in Section 4 applies only to your first successful payment for a paid plan.

Renewal charges (monthly or annual rebills) are generally non‑refundable, except where required by applicable law, or where we explicitly offer a different refund term at checkout for a specific promotion.

7. Annual subscriptions — no prorated refunds after day 30

For annual subscriptions, after day 30 from your first successful payment, we do not provide prorated refunds for unused time or unused features, except where required by applicable law.

If you qualify for a refund under Section 4, it applies only to the first billing period payment and only within the stated window and eligibility rules.

8. Partial periods / unused time (outside the guarantee window)

Outside the guarantee window in Section 4, we do not guarantee credits or refunds for partial subscription periods or unused features, unless we explicitly agree in writing or applicable law requires otherwise.

9. Taxes and currency

Prices may be presented in USD for global customers. Applicable taxes may be collected depending on your location and the checkout flow. Refund amounts may be adjusted to reflect taxes and fees as permitted by law and payment provider rules.

10. How to request a refund

Email contact@scouthook.com with:

  • the email address associated with your Scouthook account
  • the approximate date of purchase
  • a brief reason for the request (optional, but helpful)

We may ask reasonable follow‑up questions to verify purchase eligibility and prevent fraud.

11. Changes

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version.
