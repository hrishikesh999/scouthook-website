Scouthook is built around your knowledge and ideas, not templates. The hook, angle, and story are shaped from what you actually know, not from a generic prompt. The quality score also blocks AI-sounding language before you see the post. Most users feel the difference on their very first post.
What if I don't know what to post about?
That's exactly what the Idea Vault is for. Upload any document — a report, a presentation, an article you wrote — and Scouthook mines it for post-worthy insights. You'll never stare at a blank page again.
